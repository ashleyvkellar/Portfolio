UX/UI Design

Redesigned UI proposal for The Witcher

Timeline:

March 2019?

Tools:

Photoshop

Role:

UI Designer

Challenges

In The Witcher 3, I thought there were a lot of readability issues, and consistently broke the six-foot rule. In addition, I wanted to remove some irrelevant information and rework the current hierarchy, such as making the player's health the focal point by changing its location. I also wanted all to rework some of the progress bars, so that they had a consistent look and feel, rather than some being curved while others are straight.

Solution

Relocated health bar to the bottom right, balancing out from the map in the upper right corner. Action items moved closer to the player's character so they don't have to look to the corner for available actions. I also changed the location distance to be relative to the in-game location to help guide the player, rather than having it attached to the mini-map. Quests were also relocated away from the main gameplay screen to a menu.
