06 · Voice & usage rules
Do
  • Use Cormorant Garamond italic in sage for emotional emphasis
  • Pair eyebrow labels (DM Sans caps) with Cormorant headings
  • Use warm cream as the default page background always
  • Keep imagery warm-toned — parchment, earth, natural light
  • Use the AK monogram consistently in circular format
Don't
  • Use pure black (#000) — always use Warm Dark (#2D2318)
  • Use pure white backgrounds — use Warm Cream (#F5F0E8)
  • Mix in other typefaces — stick to Cormorant + DM Sans only
  • Use the sage green on colored backgrounds without checking contrast
  • Use bold (700) weight in DM Sans — max weight is 500
