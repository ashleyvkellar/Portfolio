1. Scope of Work

The Designer will provide website redesign services including UX strategy, UI design, and website implementation.

Discovery & Audit
  • Review of existing website
  • Identification of structural, usability, and content issues
UX Strategy
  • Site map (restructured navigation)
  • User flow planning (e.g., injured animal assistance, volunteering, donations)
Wireframing
  • Low-fidelity wireframes for key page templates
UI Design
  • Visual design including color, typography, and layout
  • High-fidelity mockups for approval
Website Implementation
  • Website build and setup in WordPress
  • Creation of up to 10 unique page templates
  • Population of up to 20 total pages
  • Content organization and placement (client provides content)
  • Basic functionality setup (forms, navigation, links)
Testing & Launch
  • Cross-device testing
  • Fixing broken links and usability issues
  • Final website launch
